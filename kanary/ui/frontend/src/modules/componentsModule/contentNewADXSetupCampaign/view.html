How to setup an Adx campaign
DoubleClick Ad Exchange is a real-time marketplace that helps ad networks, agency holding companies, and emerging third-party technology providers maximize ROI across millions of sites. The process to create an Adx campaign consists of three steps: (1) creating a campaign, (2) creating an ad group and targeting the ad group, then (3) creating using the DSP DoubleClick Marketing Manager.
Setup DoubleClick Adx Campaign
  1. Click the Campaigns tab. On the All online campaigns page, click the +New campaign button.
  2. Copy settings from an existing campaign or enter new settings: Next to Load settings, click Existing campaigns and choose the desired campaign.
  3. Enter a daily Budget. While this is per day, note that it represents your average spend over the month.
  4. Frequency capping: choose to cap by ad group, ad, or this campaign.
Create New Ad Group
  1. Name your new ad group.
  2. Enter a default bid. You can create bids that override the default for specific placements.
  3. Target the ad group. In the Sites and Placements section, enter placements or search for ideas.
  4. Click here to setup campaign
NOTE:- Please install X-Frame Extension to view ADX UI.
